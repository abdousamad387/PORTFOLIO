About Me

“

Every satellite pixel carries a piece of our planet's story. My mission: decode it, so every territory, on every continent, can act with intelligence.

Geospatial data speaks to those who know how to listen. I am Abdou Samad Faye, a Geo-Data Scientist bridging 25 years of planetary satellite archives and the strategic decisions that reshape territories, across Africa, Europe, Asia, and beyond.

Where others see raw images, I decode land shifts, vegetation loss signals, climate-driven risks, and urban expansion patterns, converting them into alerts, decision maps, and WebGIS applications accessible to governments, researchers, and organizations worldwide. Africa was my proving ground. The planet is my canvas.

I master complex multi-sensor environments (Landsat, Sentinel, MODIS, GEDI LiDAR) and translate fragmented data into clear models, proven predictions, and measurable outcomes. TERRASIGHT achieved 95.09% ML accuracy. AquaMind forecasts floods with 92% reliability. These are not regional achievements, they are global-grade benchmarks.

My edge? Merging scientific rigor with the power of Machine Learning and the reach of WebGIS, so every analysis becomes a living, deployed tool that drives real decisions. Wherever data exists on this planet, I can turn it into value.
